Awareness of Misinformation Tactics

Players learn to recognize how misinformation spreads — from fake news and deepfakes to misleading statistics and gossip. By experiencing these tactics in a suspenseful, game-like setting, they become more alert to the same tricks in real life.

Critical Thinking & Source Evaluation

Every clue in Verity forces players to ask: Is this reliable? Who benefits if I believe this? The puzzles are designed to sharpen critical reasoning and fact-checking skills—essential tools for navigating today's digital world.

Teamwork & Negotiation Skills

Because one player is secretly the “Disinformation Agent,” the rest must collaborate, debate, and negotiate under pressure. This strengthens communication, teamwork, and decision-making—while also making the gameplay social and exciting.

RUMOR HIGH SCHOOL

Locked inside Rumor High after dark, players must navigate eerie classrooms and shadowy hallways buzzing with gossip, half-truths, and lies. To escape, they must uncover the original source of the rumors—before misinformation and mistrust tear the group apart.
